AN OVERVIEW OF OUR PROJECT

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) stands as a complex neurodevelopmental challenge characterized by social interaction difficulties and repetitive behavior patterns. Early identification of ASD is pivotal for initiating timely interventions that can significantly enhance the quality of life for individuals affected by the disorder. However, the existing diagnosis methods are plagued by issues such as time consumption and the need for specialized professionals.
